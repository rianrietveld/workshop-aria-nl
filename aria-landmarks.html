Landmarks and roles

Landmarks give structure to your page. It's is also used to navigate and understand a page quickly.

For example:
  • <header role="banner">
  • <nav>
  • <main>
  • <aside>
  • <footer role="contentinfo">
  • <form>
  • <form role="search">
For IE 10 and less also:
  • <nav role="navigation">
  • <main role="main">
  • <aside role="complementary">
If you have more then one of the same landmark: add aria-label
  • <nav aria-label="Main navigation"> or even <nav aria-label="Main">
  • <nav aria-label="Cocial media links">
  • <aside role="Contact information">
  • <aside aria-label="Related posts">
  • <section aria-label="In the news">
Note: Only one <main> per page, with the main content.

Test with the Apple VoiceOver roter in Safari or with the Landmarks browers addon for FireFox/CHome/Opera
